Crypto Terms

CLOSE: The price at which a cryptocurrency closes at a time period.

HIGH: The highest price of a cryptocurrency of the previous session of 24 hours.
LOW: The lowest price of a cryptocurrency of the previous session of 24 hours.
VOLUME: The number of units traded in a market during a given time.
